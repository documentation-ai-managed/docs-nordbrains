## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have a clear idea of your project goals. Nordbrains specializes in custom system development, web applications, mobile apps, and advanced Shopify e-commerce solutions. Prepare a brief description of your needs, budget range, and timeline expectations.

<Callout kind="info">
  Gather these details upfront:
  - Project scope (e.g., e-commerce store, mobile app)
  - Target platforms (web, iOS/Android, Shopify)
  - Approximate budget and deadline
</Callout>

Follow these steps to kick off your project with Nordbrains efficiently.

Your Name
